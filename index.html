The Humble Historian

Lucius Montegrot is merely the humble scribe of the events he claims to have witnessed in a vision, probably induced by too much Elven wine and pipe herb. He insists that any resemblances to real persons, gods, or liches is purely coincidental and that he’s never even heard of a phoenix the size of a cathedral roof.
